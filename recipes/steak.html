Pancakes

Look at this steak

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  1. Pat your steak dry, this helps your steak form a better sear by removing the surface level moisture
  2. Rub in some salt and pepper, be generous!
  3. Get your pan smoking hot
  4. Add your steak and butter to the pan
  5. Cook until your steak has hits 52 degrees for a medium rare steak
  6. Let sit for a few minutes, it helps!
